FAQ

Get answers to commonly asked questions about Ready 3D.

How to download a model?

To download a 3D model, open the model’s page and look for the “Download” button below the viewer. If the creator has allowed downloads, you’ll see available file formats (such as .obj, .fbx, or .glb). Select your preferred format and the download will start automatically. If there’s no download option, it means the author has restricted access to the model — in that case, you can either contact the creator directly or look for similar models with open download permissions.
